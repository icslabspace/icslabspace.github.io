Money Spenk

Receipt scanner application to monitor your expenses

Money Spenk is a fun app for scanning your receipts and thus help you easily monitor your expenses, all within a juicy interface with awesome features.

Pie Care

E/M-health application

Demo of interconnecting technologies to respond to a simple need in e-Health. We've used a WebRTC based API for streaming video and audio signal, apllied some cool strategies and we've researched the tech stack needed for the final result. There are two dedicated app for the patient and the health care taker - we've affectionatelly called them toddler and nanny. All backed by a cool Clojure based server.
