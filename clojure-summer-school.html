Agenda:

Welcome to the 2nd Clojure Summer School where we'll be covering 5 days of seminars through a hands on approach to Clojure and some of its advanced topics.

 

We'll be digging into:

 

  • •Reader/evaluation 

  • •Clojure's STM 

  • •Concurrency 

  • •User datatypes 

  • •Clojure's transformation abstractions 

  • •Cool Clojure libs and APIs (asynchronous communication, web, android) 

 

The last day of this Summer School is on a Saturday and we'll be there all day :)
